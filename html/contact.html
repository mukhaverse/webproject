Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Medixa provide?

Medixa helps users check possible medication interactions and organize medicine schedules in a clear and easy way.

How does the medication interaction check work?

The user enters the medicine names, then the system uses an API to detect possible interactions and display the results clearly.

Can I get a schedule for my medicines?

Yes. The platform can generate a simple table to help organize medicine times and make daily use easier to manage.

Is the contact form available for inquiries?

Yes. Users can contact the team for support, general questions, or feedback through the contact form on this page.
