Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Medixa provide?

Medixa helps users check medication interactions, understand medication risks, and organize daily medication schedules more clearly.

How to use Medixa?

Users enter their medications, review possible interactions, and get simple guidance to manage their medicine safely and on time.

How can I contact Medixa?

You can contact Medixa through the contact form or by email during the listed working hours.

How long does it take to receive a response?

Response times may vary, but Medixa aims to reply to inquiries as soon as possible during working hours.

Is my information secure?

Medixa is designed to provide a safer and more secure user experience when accessing medication-related guidance.
